As you can see to the image on the left, the child is currently very engaged with the laptop device in front of him in which he is doing his work on. If we implement solutions like this into the education system, it will allow for students to remain engaged and have a deeper desire to learn and want to better themselves as it will be more fun for them. By creating a gamified system of education, it will allow for students to move at their own pace and feel a sense of accomplishment as they are able to conquer harder topics without being forced at a pace they won't understand.





Our Main Message:

Our main goal here is to help students have the best oppurtunity to learn the material they are currently learning. We believe that in the current education system, students are typically being forced to go at a pace that is not the best for them as it will either be too fast or too slow for them. Thus, one of the best solutions for this would be the implementation of a gamified educational system to allow them to become the masters of their own education. The implementation of gamification in STEM education would cause an overall increase in comprehension, retention, and mastery rates of the material.
